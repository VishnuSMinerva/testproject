hey vaada vaada payya en vaasam vaaangi poyaaa...

entharo mahaanu bhaavulu...

punnaara poonkuyile...

mayilaayi parannu vaa mazhavillu tholkkum en azhake...

munthirippaadam poothu nikkana munchethi ponchola.. muthu pole ente nenchil kaathuvacholam...

kanmani anbodu kaathalen naan ezhuthum kanithame...

chillujaalaka vaathilin thirasheela njoriyunnu... melleyonnu kilungiyo kaivalakal ariyaathe..
